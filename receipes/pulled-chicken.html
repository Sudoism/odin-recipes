Pulled Chicken

Description

A simple and tasteful pulled chicken done using a slow cooker

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow onion
  • 50 ml water
  • 3 msk olive oil
  • 3 msk soy
  • 3 msk chillipowder
  • 1 msk tomatopure
  • 1 msk red wine vinegear
  • salt for flavour
  • 900g chicken thigh filet

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients in the slow cooker and stir. Put the slowcooker on high heat 3 hours or low heat 5 hours.
  2. Pull apart the chicken with two forks and mix with the gravy.
  3. Serve with freshly cut onion, avocado, fresh corriander and creme freiche.
